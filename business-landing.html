TRUST AND SAFETY

The JSTP Business Verification System

To protect students and ensure every listing on JSTP comes from a legitimate employer, all businesses go through our multi-layered verification protocol before receiving a Verified Business badge.

✓ VERIFIED BUSINESS

This badge appears on your company profile and all your job listings — giving students confidence that your business is legitimate.

INITIAL VERIFICATION
→
Email Domain Verification

Businesses must register using an official company email address (e.g. @yourcompany.com). Personal Gmail or Hotmail addresses are not accepted.

→
Company Registration Number

We request and verify your official CRN or Tax ID with the Companies Office of Jamaica. This confirms your business has legal standing.

→
Website and Online Presence Check

We verify the existence of an active company website and professional social media profiles to confirm a legitimate online footprint.

ONGOING MONITORING
→
Student Reporting

Students can report suspicious listings, interactions, or potential scams directly within the platform. All reports are reviewed promptly.

→
Proactive Profile Monitoring

JSTP continuously monitors business profiles and job postings for unusual activity, generic descriptions, and red flags of fraudulent behaviour.

→
AI Anomaly Detection

AI monitors posting behaviour and communication patterns to automatically flag suspicious activity before it reaches students.

ENHANCED VERIFICATION (PREMIUM)
→
Phone Call Verification

For businesses seeking featured listings or company branding, we conduct a direct phone call to the official company number on public record.

→
Document Upload

We request official business documentation such as a business licence or recent utility bill to further confirm identity and address.
